office & residential cleaning

Let us provide you with a cleaner, healthier home, using environmentally safe products and efficient service.

Starting with cobwebs hiding in hard-to-reach corners and dust that clings to ceiling fans and light fixtures our professional house cleaners move through your home making sure every room is spotless. From the ceiling to your shelves, furniture, counters and other surfaces, including wiping surfaces to rid your home of harmful bacteria, allergens and cold-causing germs.

Our professionally trained cleaners treat delicate items with care, straightening linens and emptying wastebaskets.


Bathrooms: sanitizing and washing showers, vanities and toilets.

Kitchens: sanitizing and washing counter tops, damp-wiping cupboard fronts, tables and chairs and mopping.

Living Rooms: cleaning under couch cushions, dusting and cleaning wood and glass surfaces.
