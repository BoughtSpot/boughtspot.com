Kleen Teem Services is dedicated to cleaning offices daily with unrivalled results. A professional approach to each client’s specific cleaning requirements is the key to our success. Above all, regular visits and inspections of our clients’ premises ensures the closest attention to detail, that delights our customers.


Aſter completing an initial site survey to establish each client’s exact requirements, a free personalised proposal is provided with a comprehensive cleaning programme.


To guarantee your total security, Kleen Teem conducts a background check on all staff with the Police National Database before they are employed.


Our employees are trained in the essential aspects of the COSHH (Control of Substance Hazardous to Health). Explicit instructions are given to each employee detailing precisely what is required, to ensure maximum efficiency. New employees also undergo a retraining programme aſter an initial 12 weeks of service.


An Area Manager personally visits all sites on a regular basis whilst our employees are working and a full inspection of the premises is carried out to ensure that our standards are maintained. As an essential part of liaison, clients are asked to sign a monthly satisfaction sheet.
