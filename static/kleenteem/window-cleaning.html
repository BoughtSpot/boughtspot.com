The HighRise™ window cleaning system is the latest innovation from IPC Eagle. IPC Eagle is a leading manufacturer of cleaning equipment and tools throughout the world. This amazing machine offers a safe and effective way of cleaning multi-storey buildings and can be customised to suit specific site requirements.


The major advantage with High-Rise window cleaning is that it offers faster, cleaner and safer window cleaning. Advantages of the High-Rise window cleaning method are:

  • Completing a month’s worth of window cleaning in less than a day.
  • Safely operated from the top of buildings.
  • Cleaning windows and frames.
  • Cleaning the entire building for extended life.
  • Windows stay cleaner longer with Pure Water.
  • Pure Water removes the need to use chemicals.

SELF-CONTAINED/SELF-CLIMBING

The HighRise™ is a completely self-contained and self-climbing window cleaning system that can be operated from existing roof rigs, davit arms, roof cars or portable rigs. Operation is easy through a wireless remote control.
